About me

Hello, I’m Rodrigo Chi Durán. I hold a Ph.D. in Earth and Planetary Sciences at UC Berkeley, which is an extension of my journey that began in Electrical Engineering at the Universidad de Chile. My research revolves around geodynamics, with a particular emphasis on geomagnetism and seismology. I employ signal processing and data-driven techniques to gain insights into these areas. I’m looking forward to the opportunity to discuss and share more about my work.
